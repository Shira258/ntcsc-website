About Us

Welcome to the North Toronto computer science club! Anyone with any skill level can join, even if you have little to no experience with coding. Our club is a great environment for beginner coders, since some of our members learned the basics of coding from joining this club. Even if you don't plan on taking the computer science course, you are still welcomed to join. You can choose what parts of a project you are comfortable working on and add your own ideas in your code. We encourage our members to share ideas for projects they want to make in the club and we would love to hear some of your ideas. We hope you'll consider joining the North Toronto computer science club.
