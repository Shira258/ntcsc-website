Frequently Asked Questions

Need any help or technical support? This is the place to refer to before asking questions. We would be happy to help you in any manner necessary! If you can not find the answers you are looking for here check out our contact us page for support and more detailed information.

Question 1: How do I join Compsci Club?

Simply click on the contact us page, join the discord or show up to inperson meetings anytime and we will be happy to have you in our community

Question 2: Do I need any coding requirement to join the compsci club?

No! There is no pre-requisite information required for any grades! We are happy to teach you in our club and community. You must of course attend North Toronto Collegiate Institute and have a passion for computer science.

Question 3: What kind of commitment do I have to employ in order to be part of the club?

We would be ecstatic if you could show up to every meeting and devote a high level of attention to the club. However, if you do not have a lot of time, a low level of commitment is fine. You do not have to show up to all of the meetings and you can work on the club or participate when you have spare time.
